Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

Data Collection

Ultimate Utility Tools is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data to provide our services:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or personal details
  • No Account Required: All tools work without registration
  • Client-Side Processing: Most tools process data in your browser
  • Temporary Server Processing: Some tools may temporarily process data on our servers but do not store it

Data Storage

We do not store your data:

  • Text inputs are processed and immediately discarded
  • Uploaded files are processed in memory and not saved to disk
  • Generated results are not logged or stored
  • No user sessions or data persistence

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking:

  • No tracking cookies
  • No third-party analytics
  • Only essential cookies for website functionality
  • No cross-site tracking

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Content Delivery Network (CDN) for faster loading
  • Basic error monitoring (no personal data)
  • Web hosting infrastructure

Security

We implement security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Secure server configurations
  • Regular security updates
  • No data persistence reduces risk

Your Rights

Since we don't store personal data, there's nothing to delete or modify. You have complete control over what data you share with our tools.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our website.
